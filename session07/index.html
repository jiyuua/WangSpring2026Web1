Biography

Jin Wang is a student at CUNY Hunter College majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Media Studies. She is interested in UI/UX designs as well as web development with experience with coding languages such as Python, C++, HTML, CSS (Tailwind), and Javascript (React and p5.js). In her free time, Jin enjoys drawing and experimenting with different medias with experience in various digital art applications like Photoshop, Krita, and more. She has also created physical artworks with watercolor, markers, acrylic and oil paints. Jin hopes to further refine her skills in web development through this class and code creative and inspiring webpages.
